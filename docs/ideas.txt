## IDEA: Extreme RSI + Zone Rule (Counter-Trend Opportunity)

Description:
AI identified a counter-trend opportunity but couldn't execute because counter_trade_analysis was empty.

Example:
- Entry: $0.2811, 10 cycles later: $0.281
- Change: -0.04% (break-even)
- RSI 19.4 -> 21.9 (slight recovery started)

Proposal: Symmetrical Extreme RSI Rule

For LONG:
- RSI < 25 (Extreme oversold)
- Near support zone
- Momentum weakening
-> Evaluate counter-trend LONG opportunity

For SHORT:
- RSI > 75 (Extreme overbought)
- Near resistance zone
- Momentum weakening
-> Evaluate counter-trend SHORT opportunity

Steps:
1. Update RealMarketData to detect extreme RSI
2. Update PortfolioManager to allow counter-trend entries without medium-term alignment if RSI is extreme
3. Update prompt_json_builders.py to include extreme RSI condition in counter_trade_analysis

Requirement changes:
- Do NOT require 15m alignment (Extreme RSI is already a strong signal)
- Use low confidence (0.40-0.55) for risk reduction
